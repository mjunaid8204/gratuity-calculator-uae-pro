Calculator FAQ

Why is my resignation gratuity less?
For unlimited contract resignation, gratuity is proportional. 1/3 rate for 1-3 years, 2/3 for 3-5 years, full rate only after 5+ years. Termination always gives the full rate.
Does unpaid leave affect my gratuity?
Yes. Unpaid leave days are deducted from total service period. This tool allows you to enter unpaid leave days for an adjusted calculation.
How is partial year service calculated?
Partial years count proportionally. For example, 3 years 6 months = 3.5 years for calculation purposes. Use the "Additional Months" field above.
