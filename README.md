> ## 🔍 What is UAE Gratuity?
>
> UAE Gratuity (also called **End of Service Benefit / EOSB**) is a statutory payment that every employer must pay to an employee upon termination or resignation, provided the employee has completed at least **1 year of continuous service**.

> ## 📐 UAE Gratuity Calculation Formula (Official)
>
> ### For **Unlimited Contract** (Resignation):
> | Years of Service | Gratuity Amount |
> |-----------------|-----------------|
> | Less than 1 year | No gratuity |
> | 1 – 3 years | 1/3 of 21 days' basic salary per year |
> | 3 – 5 years | 2/3 of 21 days' basic salary per year |
> | More than 5 years | Full 21 days' basic salary per year |
>
> ### For **Unlimited Contract** (Termination by Employer):
> | Years of Service | Gratuity Amount |
> |-----------------|-----------------|
> | 1 – 5 years | 21 days' basic salary per year |
> | More than 5 years | 30 days' basic salary per year |
>
> ### For **Limited Contract**:
> - **21 days' basic salary** for each of the first 5 years
> - - **30 days' basic salary** for each year beyond 5 years
>  
>   - > ⚠️ Maximum gratuity = **2 years' total basic salary**
