🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We collect no salary data and store nothing on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

UAE Gratuity Calculator Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. Here is what we collect and what we do not:

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifying information (unless you contact us voluntarily)
  • Your salary or financial data entered into the calculator
  • Your employment details, contract type, or years of service
  • Any calculation results

What we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage data (pages visited, time on site) through Google Analytics
  • Your IP address (anonymized) for security and analytics purposes
  • Browser type and operating system for technical optimization
  • Contact form submissions if you voluntarily reach out to us

2. How We Use Information

Any anonymized data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website performance and user experience
  • Understand which articles and tools are most helpful to users
  • Fix technical bugs and errors
  • Respond to contact form inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Calculator Privacy

Our gratuity calculator operates entirely in your browser (client-side JavaScript). This means:

  • Your salary figures are never sent to our servers
  • Calculation results exist only in your browser memory
  • Closing the browser tab permanently removes all entered data
  • We have no technical ability to access what you enter in the calculator

This is a fundamental design choice. We believe your financial information should remain completely private.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies only for:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous visitor statistics (page views, session duration). You can opt out using Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Essential Cookies: Basic site functionality (e.g., remembering cookie preferences)

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of behavioral advertising.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party sites including MOHRE (mohre.gov.ae), Bayanati portal, and other government resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

UAE Gratuity Calculator Pro is intended for use by adults (18+) who are employed in the UAE. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18. If you believe a child has provided information through our site, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal personal data, your rights are straightforward:

  • Right to Know: You may contact us to ask what data (if any) we hold about you
  • Right to Deletion: Any contact form data can be deleted upon request
  • Right to Object: You may opt out of analytics cookies at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@uae-gratuity-calculator.com
  • Contact Form: contact.html
  • Response time: Within 72 hours on business days
